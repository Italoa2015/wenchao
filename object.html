    • Abandoned Objects




    This project came from one of my course during my stay in Germany last year.
    the target of this course was about photography and story-telling.
    And as a designer with a background of industril design,I am always interesting in Objects.
    We walking outside alongside the river of Bremen,they always been some stuff on the shore taken by the water.I collect them and take this series of photos. Through this photos I would like to question that "how we gonna to do with the abandoned objects when one day they come back?"

    And also I design the poster for the final exhibition.
